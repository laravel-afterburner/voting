## Overview

Eligible members submit their choices while a ballot is open. Each voter typically receives one submission per ballot unless proxy rules apply.

## Steps

1. Open **Voting** and select an open ballot, or follow a direct link from a notification.
2. Read the ballot description and any linked [Documents](/help/documents/overview).
3. Select your choice for each question.
4. Review your selections and submit.
5. Confirm the success message. You may not be able to change your vote after submission.

## Proxy votes

When your {{ entity_label }} enables proxy voting, holders of valid proxy grants vote on behalf of owners according to your configured rules. Proxy holders open **Voting → Proxy votes** to see grants and cast votes on assigned units. See [Proxy votes](/help/voting/proxy-votes) for the full workflow.
