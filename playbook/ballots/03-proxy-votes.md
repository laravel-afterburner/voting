## Overview

Proxy voting lets an owner authorize another member to vote on their behalf for a specific ballot or voting unit. Your {{ entity_label }} must enable proxy votes in **System Settings → Voting** (or equivalent) before this feature appears.

## Who can grant a proxy

Owners (or members authorized by your application's proxy rules) grant proxies for their voting units. Members with **Manage Proxy Votes** permission can assist when your bylaws allow council to record proxies on behalf of owners.

## Granting a proxy

1. Open **Voting → Proxy votes** from the main navigation.
2. Select the ballot and voting unit (for example, a lot or membership unit).
3. Choose the **Proxy holder** — must be a member of the {{ entity_label }}.
4. Submit the grant. The holder can now vote on that unit while the proxy is active.

## Casting as a proxy holder

1. Open **Voting → Proxy votes** or the ballot directly.
2. Select the unit you are voting for on behalf of the owner.
3. Cast the vote following the same steps as [Casting a vote](/help/voting/casting-a-vote). The ballot indicates when you are submitting a proxy vote.

## Revoking a proxy

Grantors and authorized administrators can **Revoke** an active proxy before the ballot closes. Once a unit has voted, the proxy cannot be used to cast a second vote for that unit.
