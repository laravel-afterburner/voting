## Overview

After a ballot closes, authorized members review results on the ballot detail page. Visibility depends on ballot settings and your role.

## Viewing results

1. Open **Voting** and select the closed ballot.
2. Open the **Results** tab or section.
3. Review vote counts per option and participation summaries.

Some ballots hide individual choices until results are published; others show live totals to administrators while voting is open.

## Exporting results

Members with **export results** permission can download a results file from the ballot page for council records or AGM minutes. Use exports in accordance with your privacy and records policies.

## Linking to meetings

When a ballot is tied to an [Events](/help/meetings/overview) session, reference the exported results in meeting minutes or attach them to [Documents](/help/documents/overview) for the official record.
